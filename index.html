New Tournament?Author: Blitz05-25-03

Well, it's quite possible that we'll be hosting the fourth installment of Paintball Cup soon. For those of you not familar with the past tournaments, we've had excellent turnout rates for all three, including: 1 vs 1, 2 vs 2, and 3 vs 3. This time, we'd like to do something a little different from the norm. We're considering the following ideas: Siege (Teams of 3 or 4 compete on a siege map [like siegecastle]), Races (Each competitor placed in a single elimination bracket, and go one on one with each other on a specially designed race map [ details to be constructed ]), 2vs2vs2vs2 (Teams of two compete on a 4-team map. Server DowntimeAuthor: XtremeBain03-09-03

As most of you have noticed the Server has had frequent downtime throughout the weekend. On Friday our provider's network was subject to attacks with such a magnitude that their entire network became unavailable to the internet for many hours Friday evening and into the early hours of Saturday. Each time they suffered attacks and our server was left virtually disconnected from the rest the internet it decided it was going to crash. Saturday morning when network connectivity was back I decided to upgrade the sendmail packages on the server to resolve a vulnerability found earlier in the week. This caused our mail system to ultimately fail. I scrambled to fix it and then the server decided to hang while booting, so I needed to get a technician working at the data center to manually check the filesystem. I just finally fixed the problem with the mail system and tested both incoming and outgoing e-mail and am pretty confident that things will work now. Another note, our provider has disabled ICMP connections due to attacks as a temporary solution so users will be unable to ping/traceroute the server until the filtering is removed which should be sometime early in this week. If you are still experiencing any difficulties just drop me an e-mail describing the situation at bain@eR33t.com.
